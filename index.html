Who we are

At Spencer we boast one of the highest support personnel to sales personnel ratio in the industry. This includes project managers, designers, and customer service providers who work directly with MFS personnel to ensure success. We are one of only a few dealers who have their own installation group in DFW. Installation is such an important part of any new facility experience how could we leave it to someone else? Our installers are ours. They are part of our team, part of our company and part of the reason we consistently hit exemplary customer service satisfaction ratings from all our customers. The Spencer Company does not out-source any of it’s service team. Everyone with Metroplex Facility Services (MFS) is a full-time certified installer or technician.

Spencer Interiors

The workforce is now comprised of four generations, each with their own unique interests and needs. Spencer’s understanding of workplace strategy optimizes company resources – space, furniture, and equipment – leading to enhanced worker satisfaction and workplace performance.

Spencer Construction

We not only install new furniture, we also offer a full menu of interior finishes for our products and services. The Spencer Construction division specializes in architectural interiors, such as movable walls, access flooring, plug & play technology and lighting.

Spencer Care

Spencer Care is a division of the Spencer Company dedicated to providing the best quality furniture to healthcare professionals. We provide quality, cost-effective furniture that is compliant, meeting the demands of the healthcare industry. Furniture options are selected based on project budget, durability, ease of replacement parts and cleaning requirements, assisting in the control of infectious disease.

Metroplex Facility Services

Metroplex Facility Services (MFS) is wholly owned by The Spencer Company, providing any required furniture services. With a 65,000 square foot warehouse they are set up to receive and store, new and used products for any length of time.

What we do

Preliminary Review & Design

Interior Construction

We not only install new furniture, we also offer a full menu of interior finishes for our products and services. This allows us to be a single source for many of your construction needs; shortening your construction time, costs, and increasing your efficiency, so you and your employees can get right to work.

Our certified installation crews are the best of the best. They will meet at the job site to check, unload, and stage product before the actual install begins. We also offer a service program to provide cleaning, adjusting, or treating of furniture to extend its life cycle so your office always has a great first impression.

Guaranteed Asset Protection

We have the best warehouse with approximately 65,000 square feet of space to securely receive, inspect to be of the highest quality, and store. You will always have the highest quality furniture delivered your new or existing space.

Haworth

Haworth is a major influencer in design and manufacturing of office furniture, architectural products and technology on a global scale. They encompass over 6,000 team members that operate in 126 countries around the globe.  Haworth reported sales of $ 1.41 billion in 2013, making them one of the largest manufacturers of office interior products in the world. By infusing product designs using seven key design logics, and combining a deep understanding of human, organizational, and facility performance, they created the “Integrated Palette”—a customized workspace that is truly more integrated, more adaptable, and more sustainable. A simple kit of parts can come together to create an entire office environment that supports their client’s culture and workstyles. The Spencer Company is a Preferred Haworth Dealer which assures the best pricing and support available for our clients.

About Spencer

The Spencer Company was founded in 1977, to be a complete resource in providing solutions to our customers. Our services include office furnishings selection/procurement, design, project management, and installation. We now offer interior construction and architectural products for a more turnkey solution.

Our company’s reputation for extraordinary customer service, earned the organization the achievement of becoming a Haworth Preferred Dealer. In addition, we offer a large array of niche product solutions that span broad spectrum of vertical markets, from corporate and higher education to health care.

The Spencer Company is strong and stable. Through 30 + years and three recessions we have remained financially solvent with solid practices, enabling us to serve our customers with confidence. That stability extends to our management team, which has more than 211 cumulative years of industry experience.
